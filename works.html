Level & Monster Designer

I am responsible for the design, production, and improvement of in-game modes and levels, including maps, gameplay, monsters, their skills, and AI, and so on.


Monster & Boss Design

First, the narrative designer will work with me to design the monster's characteristics. After this, we give the result to the concept artist as well as the modeling artist. I will design the monster's skills, behavior patterns, and the duration of each action. Then in collaboration with the animator, special effects artist, and sound engineer, we will create all the resources I need.

Level Design

First, I will work with the narrative designer to design the backstory of the map. After that, I will work on the first version of the level model and give it to the scenario artist to continue creating art resources.

Realization

After the monster art resources are returned to me, I need to add AI to the monster in the game engine and integrate all the resources for making the monster. The game scenes need me to write the flow and set up the monsters in order to turn them into real game levels.
